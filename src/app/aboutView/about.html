Fixing Fixed Hardware since 2015

Mission

Our goal is to provide a platform for sharing information about potentially dangerous routes that need to be rebolted and offer an easy way to donate to the cause.

Background

Many of America's sports routes were bolted in the 80s and 90s by climbing visionaries living on the cheap. A few dacades later many of these bolts have naturally weathered and rusted and need to be replaced.

F.A.Q.s

Search for climbing areas on the crags page. You can add climbs that need to be rebolted from any climbing area's page. You can also rate how bad a climb needs attention

You can donate to a specific climb by using the search function on a climbin area and clicking the donate button.

Once you've donated make sure you spread the word! This is a team effort!

All money goes directly towards hardware so those devoting their time to re-equip routes do not have to burden all of the cost
